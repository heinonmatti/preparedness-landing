About

These tools are part of the M-LIFE (Meso-Level Influence Framework and Explorable) research project, which investigates the meso-level building blocks of community preparedness — the practices, norms, tools, and structures that sit between institutional policy and individual action.

The actant framework follows Dave Snowden's actor/constraint/constructor ontology, informed by Alicia Juarrero's work on constraints and Constructor Theory. PNI methodology follows Cynthia Kurtz.

Project by Matti T.J. Heino.
